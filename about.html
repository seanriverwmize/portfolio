This page is about my story. To see my resume, take a look at the Projects page.

I've been fascinated by technology for as long as I can remember. Some of my earliest memories include internally engineering my own toys, contemplating the incredible capabilities of the television, and being in awe at the sheer complexity of the video games I played. I carried this fascination with me throughout the entirety of my childhood (and beyond), but only as a consumer, not a creator. That is, until I met Soni.

I couldn't have known when I first met Soni how grateful most junior engineers would be to learn from a Senior Enginner at the Google.
